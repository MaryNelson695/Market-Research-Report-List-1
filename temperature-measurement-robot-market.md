<p><p>The temperature measurement robot market includes two primary types: infrared imaging and thermal imaging. Infrared imaging utilizes infrared sensors to detect temperature variations by capturing infrared radiation emitted from objects, providing real-time temperature assessments. Thermal imaging, on the other hand, produces visual representations of temperature distributions, allowing for more detailed analysis by imaging heat patterns. Both technologies are essential for applications such as industrial monitoring, medical diagnostics, and safety inspections, enhancing accuracy and efficiency in temperature assessment.</p></p>
