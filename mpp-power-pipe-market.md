<p><p>The MPP Power Pipe market is segmented into two main types: Excavation and Non-excavation. Excavation-type methods involve digging trenches to install pipes, often used for large-scale projects where direct underground access is needed. In contrast, non-excavation methods employ techniques like horizontal directional drilling or pipe bursting, minimizing surface disruption. These non-intrusive methods are gaining popularity due to their cost-effectiveness and reduced environmental impact, making them suitable for urban areas and sensitive landscapes.</p></p>
