PVDF granule is a higher-density form, suitable for applications requiring enhanced mechanical properties and durability, such as solar panel frames and components. In contrast, PVDF powder is often used for coatings and films, offering flexibility and ease of processing. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing the efficiency and longevity of photovoltaic systems, contributing to renewable energy solutions.</p></p>
