<p><strong>The Aluminum Gratings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Press-locked Gratings</li><li>Swage-locked Gratings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum gratings market encompasses various types, notably press-locked and swage-locked gratings. Press-locked gratings are created by pressing the load-bearing bars into the crossbars, providing a strong, durable structure ideal for heavy loads. In contrast, swage-locked gratings utilize a swaging process to lock the bars together, offering high strength and resistance to distortion. Both types are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and widely used in industrial, commercial, and architectural applications, promoting safety and efficiency in various environments.</p></p>
