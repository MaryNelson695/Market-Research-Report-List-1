<p><ul><li>Rising Film Evaporator</li><li>Falling Film Evaporator</li><li>Film Scraping Evaporator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The BM Series Film Evaporator market comprises three main types: Rising Film Evaporators, Falling Film Evaporators, and Film Scraping Evaporators. Rising Film Evaporators utilize upward flow, promoting effective heat transfer and rapid vaporization, ideal for heat-sensitive materials. Falling Film Evaporators employ gravity for liquid flow down the heated surface, enhancing efficiency for low-viscosity liquids. Film Scraping Evaporators feature mechanical scrapers that remove concentrated liquid, preventing fouling and maximizing heat transfer, suitable for highly viscous products. Each type serves specific industrial applications efficiently.</p></p>
