<p><strong>The Ultra Heat Treatment (UHT) System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Heating UHT System</li><li>Indirect Heating UHT System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultra Heat Treatment (UHT) system market is primarily categorized into two types: direct heating and indirect heating systems. Direct heating UHT systems involve direct contact between the heating medium and the product, ensuring rapid temperature rise and effective sterilization. In contrast, indirect heating UHT systems utilize heat exchangers to transfer heat without direct contact, preserving product integrity and flavor. Both types are essential for extending shelf life and maintaining the quality of liquid food products through efficient thermal processing.</p></p>
