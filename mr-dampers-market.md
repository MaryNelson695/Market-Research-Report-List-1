<p><p>The MR dampers market is categorized into active and semi-active dampers, each serving distinct roles in vibration control. Active dampers utilize external power sources to adjust their properties dynamically, offering superior control and responsiveness for applications like automotive suspension and seismic protection. In contrast, semi-active dampers rely on internal adjustments, responding to changes in conditions while consuming minimal energy, striking a balance between performance and efficiency. Both types enhance stability and comfort, making them vital in various engineering fields.</p></p>
