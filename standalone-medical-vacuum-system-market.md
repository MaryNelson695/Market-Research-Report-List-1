Dry Claw Pumps utilize two rotating claws to create a vacuum without lubricants, ensuring cleaner operation. Dry Rotary Vane Technology operates similarly but employs rotating vanes in a casing, also without oil, while Oil Sealed Rotary Vane Technology uses oil for lubrication, enhancing efficiency but requiring more maintenance. Each technology serves distinct medical applications, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
