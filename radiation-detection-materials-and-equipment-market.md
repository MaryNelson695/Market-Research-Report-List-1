<p><ul><li>Gas-Filled Detectors</li><li>Scintillators Detectors</li><li>Solid-State Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiation detection materials and equipment market consists of three primary types: gas-filled detectors, scintillator detectors, and solid-state detectors. Gas-filled detectors utilize ionization of gases to measure radiation levels, offering high sensitivity and versatility. Scintillator detectors employ materials that emit light when exposed to radiation, providing quick and accurate readings ideal for various applications. Solid-state detectors use semiconductor materials to directly convert radiation energy into electronic signals, enabling precise measurement and compact designs suitable for advanced detection needs.</p></p>
