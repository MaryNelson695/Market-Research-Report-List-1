<p><p>The HDPE extrusion line market consists of two main types: single-layer and multilayer systems. Single-layer extrusion lines produce uniform products from a single type of material, making them suitable for applications requiring simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, multilayer extrusion lines enable the integration of multiple materials, enhancing product functionality, barrier properties, and overall performance. This versatility caters to various industries, including packaging, construction, and consumer goods, as multilayer systems can create complex structures with distinct attributes.</p></p>
