<p><ul><li>Linear Image Sensors</li><li>Area Image Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OPF CMOS sensors market is primarily divided into two types: linear image sensors and area image sensors. Linear image sensors capture images one line at a time and are commonly used in applications like scanning and barcode reading, offering high-speed processing. In contrast, area image sensors capture images in two dimensions simultaneously, making them ideal for photography, video recording, and surveillance. Both types benefit from advancements in CMOS technology, enhancing image quality, sensitivity, and operational efficiency.</p></p>
