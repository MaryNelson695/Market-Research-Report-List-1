A dual-axis robot arm offers movement across two different joints, providing enhanced flexibility and precision for tasks like welding and painting. In contrast, a single-axis robot arm operates on one joint, suitable for simpler applications such as pick-and-place operations. Each type meets specific automation needs, with dual-axis arms ideal for more complex tasks, while single-axis arms cater to straightforward, repetitive actions in various industries.</p></p>
