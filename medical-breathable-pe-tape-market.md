<p><p>The medical breathable PE tape market is categorized into two main types: Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE) and High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE). LDPE is softer and more flexible, making it ideal for applications requiring comfort and conformability on the skin. In contrast, HDPE is stiffer and offers superior strength and barrier properties, making it suitable for more demanding applications. Both types play integral roles in medical settings, providing reliable adhesion while allowing skin to breathe, ensuring both comfort and hygiene for patients.</p></p>
