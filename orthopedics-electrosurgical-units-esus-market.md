<p><strong>The Orthopedics Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopole Electrosurgical Units</li><li>Bipolar Electrosurgical Units</li><li>Vessel Sealing Units</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Orthopedics Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) market comprises various types, including monopole, bipolar, and vessel sealing units. Monopole ESUs utilize a single active electrode for cutting and coagulation, suitable for larger surgical areas. Bipolar ESUs employ two electrodes to control current more precisely, minimizing thermal damage and enhancing safety in delicate procedures. Vessel sealing units specifically target blood vessels, providing secure sealing without the need for clamps or sutures, promoting efficient and effective surgical outcomes in orthopedic procedures.</p></p>
