<p><ul><li>Surface Suction</li><li>Submersible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PV water pumps market is categorized primarily into surface suction and submersible types. Surface suction pumps are installed above ground and draw water from a source using suction, making them ideal for shallow water applications. In contrast, submersible pumps operate underwater, pushing water to the surface, making them suitable for deeper wells and tough conditions. Both types leverage photovoltaic technology, allowing for efficient and sustainable water extraction, particularly in remote or off-grid locations.</p></p>
