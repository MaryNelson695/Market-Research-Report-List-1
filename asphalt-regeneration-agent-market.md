<p><ul><li>Heat-In-Place Regeneration</li><li>Plant Mix Hot Regeneration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Asphalt Regeneration Agent market comprises two primary types: Heat-In-Place Regeneration and Plant Mix Hot Regeneration. </p><p>Heat-In-Place Regeneration involves recycling existing asphalt on-site using specialized equipment that heats the pavement to restore its original properties, allowing for immediate reuse. In contrast, Plant Mix Hot Regeneration entails processing reclaimed asphalt at a facility, where it is mixed with new materials and heated before being repaved. Both methods aim to enhance asphalt durability while promoting sustainability in road maintenance.</p></p>
