<p><p>The solvent-based 3C coating market features various types, primarily thermosetting coatings and UV curing coatings. Thermosetting coatings harden upon heating and form strong, durable films, making them ideal for protective and decorative applications. In contrast, UV curing coatings undergo a rapid curing process when exposed to ultraviolet light, resulting in efficient production and reducing volatile organic compound emissions. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, aerospace, and electronics, enhancing durability and appearance while meeting regulatory standards.</p></p>
