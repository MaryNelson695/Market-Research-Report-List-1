<p><ul><li>Absolute Angle Measuring Encoders</li><li>Incremental Angle Measuring Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The angle measuring encoder market is primarily divided into two types: absolute and incremental encoders. Absolute angle measuring encoders provide a unique position value for each angle, ensuring reliable measurements even after power loss. They are ideal for applications requiring precise position tracking. Conversely, incremental angle measuring encoders generate relative position changes, offering high speed and resolution but requiring a reference point after power loss. Both types serve distinct industrial needs, enhancing automation and control in various applications.</p></p>
