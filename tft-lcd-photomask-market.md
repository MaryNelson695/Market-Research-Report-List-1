<p><strong>The TFT-LCD Photomask Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Soda-lime</li><li>Synthetic Quartz</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TFT-LCD photomask market comprises two primary types: soda-lime and synthetic quartz. Soda-lime photomasks are cost-effective and suitable for low-end applications but have limitations in optical clarity and thermal stability. In contrast, synthetic quartz photomasks offer superior optical properties, increased durability, and higher resistance to environmental factors, making them ideal for advanced display technologies. The choice between these types depends on specific application requirements, cost considerations, and desired performance outcomes in TFT-LCD manufacturing processes.</p></p>
