Low Frequency RF Sensors operate typically below 30 kHz and are often used in applications like industrial automation and transportation tracking. Mid Frequency RF Sensors, ranging from 30 kHz to 300 MHz, are commonly utilized in RFID systems and some wireless communication. High Frequency RF Sensors, exceeding 300 MHz, are leveraged for advanced applications such as IoT devices, communications, and surveillance, enabling efficient data transmission and monitoring.</p></p>
