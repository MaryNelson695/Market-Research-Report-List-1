<p><p>The refrigeration inorganic phase change materials (PCMs) market is categorized into Type I and Type II. Type I PCMs include salts and their hydrates, which typically have high thermal conductivity and are effective in temperature regulation. Type II PCMs consist of metal alloys and other non-hydrated salts, often offering broader temperature ranges and improved stability. Both types are utilized for thermal energy storage and temperature control in various applications, including building energy management and cold chain logistics, enhancing efficiency and sustainability.</p></p>
