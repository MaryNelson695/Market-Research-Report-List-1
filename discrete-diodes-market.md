<p><strong>The Discrete Diodes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The discrete diodes market comprises various types, each serving distinct functions. Schottky diodes are known for their fast switching speeds and low forward voltage drop, making them ideal for high-frequency applications. Transient Suppression Diodes (TVS) protect circuits from voltage spikes. Commutation Diodes facilitate the rapid switching of inductive loads in circuits, preventing voltage backflow. Other types encompass rectifier and Zener diodes, broadening applications across consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial sectors, enhancing circuit efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
