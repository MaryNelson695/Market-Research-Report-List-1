<p><strong>The Medical Grade Hydroxypropyl Cellulose Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>L-HPC</li><li>H-HPC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical grade hydroxypropyl cellulose market is primarily divided into two types: L-HPC (Low Density Hydroxypropyl Cellulose) and H-HPC (High Density Hydroxypropyl Cellulose). L-HPC is commonly used as a binder and disintegrant in pharmaceuticals due to its low viscosity and good flow properties. In contrast, H-HPC offers enhanced viscosity and is predominantly utilized as a thickening agent and stabilizer in various formulations. Both grades are essential in ensuring optimal drug delivery, stability, and bioavailability.</p></p>
