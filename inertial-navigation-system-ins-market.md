<p><p>Inertial Navigation Systems (INS) utilize various technologies to determine an object's position and orientation without external references. Mechanical gyro technology relies on spinning mass gyroscopes for navigation. Ring laser gyro technology employs laser beams to detect rotation with high precision. Fiber optics gyro technology uses light in optical fibers for angular velocity measurement, providing superior accuracy. MEMS technology miniaturizes sensors, offering cost-effective and compact solutions for navigation. Each type caters to specific applications across aerospace, automotive, and military sectors.</p></p>
